Alarm System - No Hood Switch in Alarm Kit

SI B 65 20 06
October 2006

Sound System, Cruise, Alarms, Monitors
Technical Service

This Service Information bulletin supersedes S.M. B65 225 04 dated August 2004.

SUBJECT
No Hood Switch in Alarm Kit

MODEL
E83 (X3)

SITUATION
The hood switch is no longer supplied with X3 alarm kit, P/N 65 73 0 305 698

CAUSE
US specification X3 vehicles are 100% pre-wired (including hood switch) for alarm installation.

PROCEDURE
No action necessary. The hood switch previously supplied with the alarm kit is not required.
