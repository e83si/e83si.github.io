Front Seat, Comfort




52 17 390 - Removing and installing or replacing front left or right head restraints (comfort)





Pull headrest upwards firmly.
If necessary, remove rear panel at top and unclip head restraint from mount.





Installation:
Remove rear panel at top.
Insert head restraint in backrest, then clip rods (1) of head restraint into head restraint mount (2).
Carry out function check.
