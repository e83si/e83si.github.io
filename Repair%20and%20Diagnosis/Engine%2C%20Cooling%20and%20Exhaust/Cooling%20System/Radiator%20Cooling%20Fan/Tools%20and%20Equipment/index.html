Radiator Cooling Fan: Tools and Equipment




Hub Holder

AST tool# BMW 2200





For holding the water pump hub during removal of fan clutch. Used in conjunction with tools S 10 32X36 and 8032. Applicable for all BMW engines with 32mm fan clutch nut. Excluding engines M42,M50, and M70.
