13 61 050 Replace Control Unit




13 61 050 - Replace control unit





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Follow instructions for removing and installing control units Service and Repair
- Read out fault memory of DME/DDE control unit
- Switch off ignition





Release screws.
Remove electronics box cover (1).

Installation:
Engage electronics box cover (1) correctly in hinges.





Unlock plug (1) and detach from control unit (2).
Unlock and remove control unit (2).





Add final details to vehicle.
If replacing control units, carry out coding/programming Programming and Relearning.





Note:
Now clear the fault memory.
