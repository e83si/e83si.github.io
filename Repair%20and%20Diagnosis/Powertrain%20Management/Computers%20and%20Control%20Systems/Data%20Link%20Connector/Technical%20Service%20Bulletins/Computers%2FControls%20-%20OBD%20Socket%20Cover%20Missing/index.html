Computers/Controls - OBD Socket Cover Missing

SI B 61 09 07
General Electrical Systems

August 2007
Technical Service

SUBJECT
OBD Socket Housing Cover Missing

MODEL
E90, E91, E92, E93 (3 Series) vehicles produced from 9/07
E60, E61 (5 Series) vehicles produced from 3/07
E65, E66 (7 Series) vehicles produced from 9/07
E83 (X3) vehicles produced from 3/07

SITUATION
The housing cover of the OBD socket is missing.

CAUSE
The housing cover has been discontinued

PROCEDURE
Do not install parts.

The housing cover of the OBD socket has been discontinued due to legal regulations in the USA.

It is no longer necessary to install the socket housing.

WARRANTY INFORMATION
For information only
