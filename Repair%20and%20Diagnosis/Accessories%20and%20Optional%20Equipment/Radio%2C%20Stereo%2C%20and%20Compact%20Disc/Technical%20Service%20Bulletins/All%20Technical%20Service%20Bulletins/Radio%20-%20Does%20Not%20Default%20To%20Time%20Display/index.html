Radio - Does Not Default To Time Display

SI B65 20 07
Sound System, Cruise, Alarms, Monitors

August 2007
Technical Service

SUBJECT
Radio Does not Default to "Time" Display

MODEL
E83 * (X3) from 8/31/06 to 2/28/07

E85, E86 * (Z4) from 8/31/06 to 04/01/07

*Only with option 662 (Business CD radio)

SITUATION
After cycling the key, the radio does not default to "Time" display after showing the radio display for a short period (6 seconds) when the function was selected by pressing the "clock" button on the radio.

CAUSE
Software error in the Business CD radio.

CORRECTION
Replace the radio and install an improved Business CD radio.





PARTS INFORMATION

WARRANTY INFORMATION
Covered under the terms of the BMW New Vehicle Limited Warranty.

If the appropriate labor operation is not contained in KSD, then a work time labor operation should be used.
