Alarm Horn: Service and Repair




65 75 055 - Removing and installing/replacing emergency power siren with tilt sensor





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove heater bulkhead 51 71 080 Removing and Installing/Replacing Heater Bulkhead.





Unfasten plug connection (1) and disconnect.
Release screw (2) and remove emergency power siren (3) with emergency power siren holder in direction of arrow.

Installation:
Make sure emergency power siren holder is in correct installation position.





Release nut (1) and remove emergency power siren (2) from holder (3).
