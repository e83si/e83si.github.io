Parking Assist System - Paint Peeling On PDC Sensor

SI B 66 05 07
Distance Systems, Cruise Control, Remote Control

October 2007
Technical Service

SUBJECT
Paint Peeling from PDC Sensor

MODEL
E70 (X5), E83 (X3) vehicles produced September 1, 2006 to February 22, 2007

SITUATION
Paint partly or fully peels from the PDC (Park Distance Control) sensors. In the case of partial paint peeling or blistering, this may interfere with the operation of the PDC system.

Part numbers affected:

^ 66 20 9 127 800
^ 66 20 9 127 801

CAUSE
Faulty painting process of PDC sensor. Stocks were cleared August 2007

CORRECTION
In case of a customer complaint, replace all the PDC sensors on the affected bumper.

PARTS INFORMATION

Part Number Description Quantity

Refer to EPC PDC sensor 4

WARRANTY INFORMATION
Covered under the terms of the BMW New Vehicle Limited Warranty.

Please refer to the latest KSD for all applicable labor operations and allowances.

If the appropriate labor operation is not contained in KSD, then a work time labor operation should be used.
