Replacing Light Bulb for Footwell Light




63 99 437 - Replacing light bulb for footwell light





Warning!
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (interior lights) Instructions for Replacing Light Bulbs (Interior Lights).





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove footwell light Removing and Installing/Replacing Footwell Light





Version A:
Unlock catch (1) on footwell light (2).
Remove cover cap (3) in direction of arrow.





Remove bulb (1) from bulb holder (2) in direction of arrow.





Version B:
Turn bulb holder (1) in direction of arrow and pull out of footwell light (2).

Installation Note:
Make sure bulb holder (1) is correctly engaged on footwell light (2).





Remove bulb (1) from bulb holder (2) in direction of arrow.





Installation Note:
Note type of bulb Interior Lights.
