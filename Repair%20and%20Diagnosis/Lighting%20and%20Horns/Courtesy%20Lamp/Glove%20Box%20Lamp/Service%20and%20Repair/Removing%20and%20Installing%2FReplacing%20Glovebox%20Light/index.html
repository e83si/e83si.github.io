Removing and Installing/Replacing Glovebox Light




63 31 150 - Removing and installing/replacing glovebox light





Important!
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (interior lights) Instructions for Replacing Light Bulbs (Interior Lights).





Lever glovebox light (1) with suitable tool in direction of arrow out of glovebox.
Disconnect plug connection and remove glovebox light (1).

If necessary, remove bulb Replacing Bulb for Glovebox Light.
