Turning Light (LM Lights Module With Ahl Adaptive Directional Headlights)



AHL Adaptive Headlight

Functional description, see BMW Technology:
TIS -> Document -> SI Technology -> SBT Number -> 63 03 03 047

WARNING: When you are working on the bi-xenon lights, observe the accident prevention regulations. The headlight system has dangerous high voltage.

To switch on the Adaptive Headlight:
- Switch on terminal 15
- Turn the light switch to position "A"
- Darken the rain / driving light sensor

NOTE: Depending on the coding, the Headlight moves only in one direction or not at all when stationary.
