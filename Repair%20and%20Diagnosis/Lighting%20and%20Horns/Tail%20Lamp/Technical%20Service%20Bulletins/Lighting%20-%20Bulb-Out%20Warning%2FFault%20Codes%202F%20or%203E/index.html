Lighting - Bulb-Out Warning/Fault Codes 2F or 3E

SI B 63 06 08
Lights

July 2008
Technical Service

SUBJECT
Bulb-Out Warning, FC 2F or 3E

MODEL
E83 (X3 3.0si) produced from August 2006

SITUATION
The customer complains that the bulb-out warning is displayed in the instrument cluster. Diagnosis shows fault 2F or 3E (Rear fog light [US adaptive brake light] right or left faulty) is stored.

INFORMATION
The fault is in the adaptive brake light circuit (bulb, bulb holder or wiring) located in the inner section of the rear lamp cluster (in the rear hatch). Use the available test plan in the diagnosis equipment to diagnose and repair the fault.

WARRANTY INFORMATION
Information only
