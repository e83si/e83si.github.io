Removing and Installing/Replacing Left Headlight (Xenon Headlight)




63 12 010 - Removing and installing or replacing left headlight (xenon headlight)





Warning!
Version with xenon headlights: Danger to life due to high voltage! Therefore, before removing, disconnect all components from the power supply (lighting system and ignition off).
Work on the entire xenon lighting system (control unit, ignition unit with bulb) may only be carried out by specialist personnel.
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (exterior lights) Instructions for Handling Light Bulbs (Exterior Lights).





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove front bumper trim
Version with M aerodynamic kit:
- Partially remove headlight bracket 51 11 710 - Removing and installing/replacing left or right headlight bracket (M Technic aerodynamic kit)





Disconnect plug connection (1).
Release screws (2). Tightening torque 63 12 1AZ [1][2]Headlamp.
Release screws (3). Tightening torque 63 12 9AZ [1][2]Headlamp.
Remove headlamp (4) in direction of arrow.





Installation Note:
Install headlight (1) so that locators (2) fit exactly on guides (3).
Observe gap dimensions.
Adjust headlight Adjusting Headlights.





Replacement:
- Remove ignition unit/bulb for xenon headlight
- Remove control unit for xenon headlights
- If necessary, remove bulbs.
