Replacing Bulb for Front Left Side Light (Xenon Headlight)




63 99 155 - Replacing bulb for front left side light (xenon headlight)





Warning!
Version with xenon headlights: Danger to life due to high voltage! Therefore, before removing, disconnect all components from the power supply (lighting system and ignition off).
Work on the entire xenon lighting system (control unit, ignition unit with bulb) may only be carried out by specialist personnel.
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (exterior lights) Instructions for Handling Light Bulbs (Exterior Lights).

Replacing bulb for side light, front left Replacing Bulb for Side Light, Front Left

Note:
Headlight (2) removed for purposes of clarity.

Disconnect associated plug connection.
Turn bulb for side light (1) in direction of arrow and remove from headlight (2).

Installation Note:
Make sure sealing ring (3) is correctly seated on side light bulb (1).
Make sure side light bulb (1) is correctly engaged in headlight (2).
