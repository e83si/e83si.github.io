Replacing Bulb for Front Ceiling Light




63 99 431 - Replacing bulb for front ceiling light





Warning!
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (interior lights) Instructions for Replacing Light Bulbs (Interior Lights).





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove front ceiling light Service and Repair





Version without inside reading lamp:
Release screws (1) and remove lens.





Remove festoons (1) from lens (2).

Installation Note:
Note type of bulb Interior Lights.





Version with inside reading lamp:
Turn bulb holder in direction of arrow and remove from ceiling light.





Remove bulb (1) from bulb holder (2) in direction of arrow.

Installation Note:
Note type of bulb Interior Lights.
