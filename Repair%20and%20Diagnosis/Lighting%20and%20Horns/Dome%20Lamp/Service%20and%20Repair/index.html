Dome Lamp: Service and Repair




63 31 000 - Removing and installing/replacing complete ceiling light (front)





Special tools required:
- 00 9 317 00 9 317 Trim Panel Wedge





Important!
Follow instructions for handling light bulbs (interior lights) Instructions for Replacing Light Bulbs (Interior Lights).





Lever out ceiling light (1) with special tool 00 9 317 00 9 317 Trim Panel Wedge as illustrated.

Disconnect associated plug connection and remove ceiling light (1).





Replacement:

- If necessary, convert bulbs.
- Note bulb type Interior Lights.
