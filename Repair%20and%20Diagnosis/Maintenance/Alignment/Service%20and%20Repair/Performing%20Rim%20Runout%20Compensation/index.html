Performing Rim Runout Compensation




32 00 ... - Performing rim runout compensation





Note:
Rim runout compensation involves electronically recording the lateral runout of the rim and the possible clamping error of the quick-clamping unit for one wheel rotation and compensating the toe and camber for measurement/alignment.

Raise body.
Perform rim runout compensation in accordance with equipment manufacturer's instructions.
Compress/deflect car.
