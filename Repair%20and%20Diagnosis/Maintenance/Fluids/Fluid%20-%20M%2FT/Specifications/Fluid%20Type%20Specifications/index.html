Fluid Type Specifications




Manual Transmission Fluid

All Models except listed below
Type MTF-LT2
BMW Part No. 83 22 0 309 031
This fluid is not to be used in the Dual-Clutch Manual Transmission (M DCT Drive logic).


From Model Year 2006 all vehicles equipped with a manual transmission and the N52 engine require the MTF-LT3 lifetime fluid.
N52 equipped vehicles MTF-LT3
BMW Part No. 83 22 0 409 878
