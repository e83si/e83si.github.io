75000 MI or 120000 KM

Lubricate
    Wheel Hub
      Grease wheel centering hubs.
Reset
    Service Reminder Indicators
      Reset Service Interval Indicator. NOTE: Do not reset Service Indicator when additional oil changes (between the normal intervals) are requested by the customer.
Clean
    Brake Caliper
      Clean brake pad contact points in calipers.
Check
    Parking Brake System
      Check parking brake actuation. Adjust if necessary. Burnish parking brake.
    Brakes and Traction Control
      Check overall thickness of front and rear disc brake pads (outer pads only) using special tool. Record values. Examine brake disc surfaces. Check thickness of parking brake linings only when replacing rear pads. Record values.
Replace
    Oil Filter, Engine
      Change oil filter while engine is at operating temperature.
    Engine Oil
      Change oil while engine is at operating temperature. We recommend BMW High Performance Synthetic oil, p/n 07 51 0 017 866.
    Cabin Air Filter / Purifier
      Replace microfilter. NOTE: reduce replacement interval in dusty operating conditions.
