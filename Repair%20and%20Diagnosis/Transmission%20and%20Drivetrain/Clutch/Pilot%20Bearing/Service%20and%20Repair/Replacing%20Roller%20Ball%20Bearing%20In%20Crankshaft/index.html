Replacing Roller Ball Bearing In Crankshaft




11 21 571 - Replacing roller ball bearing in crankshaft (N52K)





Special tools required:
- 00 5 500 00 5 500 Handle for Drifts
- 11 2 340
- 11 2 350





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove clutch.





Remove guide bearing with special tool 11 2 340.





Install new thrust bearing and drive firmly home with special tool 11 2 350 in conjunction with special tool 00 5 500 00 5 500 Handle for Drifts.





Assemble engine.
