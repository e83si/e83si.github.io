General Reference To SBT

Functional description, see BMW Technology:
TIS -> Document -> SI Technology -> SBT Number
- 2701 03 048

The transfer case VTG is part of the xDrive all-wheel drive system. The VTG handles the stepless ignition distribution of input torques to the front and rear axles. The ignition distribution takes place in line with requirements by means of an electronically controlled multi-plate clutch.
