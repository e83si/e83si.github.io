Activation of Central Locking



Activation Of Central Locking

The following points should be observed when activating the drives of the central locking system via the BMW diagnostic system:

Driver's door

Selection Action
Lock door without central arrest Driver's door is locked
Lock door with central arrest Central arrest on all doors
Unlock door All doors are unlocked

Front passenger's door and rear doors

Selection Action
Lock door without central arrest Front passenger door and rear doors are locked
Lock door with central arrest Front passenger door and rear doors are arrested Driver's door is arrested if it was locked beforehand.
Unlock door All doors are unlocked

Test
The status of the doors can be checked as follows:

Selection Test
Lock door without central arrest Door is locked however, it can be opened by pulling the inner door handle twice
Lock door with central arrest The door can be opened neither with the outer door handle nor with the inner door handle
