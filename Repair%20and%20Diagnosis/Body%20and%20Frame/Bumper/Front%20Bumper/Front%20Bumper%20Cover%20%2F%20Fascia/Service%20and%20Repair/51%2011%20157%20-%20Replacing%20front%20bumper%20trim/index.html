51 11 157 - Replacing front bumper trim





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Unscrew and remove license plate
- Remove holder for license plate Service and Repair.
- Remove front bumper trim.
- Remove upper section of bumper trim
- Remove lower section of bumper trim
Build date from 09/06:
- If necessary, remove side marker
- If necessary, remove spray nozzles
- If necessary, remove ultrasonic transducer Removing and Installing/Replacing an Ultrasonic Transducer at Front (Inside) (Park Distance Control)
