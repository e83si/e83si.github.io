Trunk / Liftgate: Service and Repair




41 62 000 - Removing and installing rear lid

Read contents of Body, General Service Precautions.
Refer to texts on KSD CD (flat rate number 41 62 000) for details of stripping and rigging tasks.





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove trim for rear lid
- Disconnect all plug and socket cable connections on rear lid.





Open screws (1) on rear lid hinge to rear lid.
Remove trunk lid.
