Removing and Installing/Replacing Door Brake on Left or Right Front Door




51 21 280 - Removing and installing or replacing door brake on left or right front door





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Close door windows
- Remove sound insulation Removing and Installing/Replacing Sound Insulation in Left or Right Front Door in front area only





Release screw (1) on door retainer (2).
Lever out seal (3) in direction of arrow.





Note:
Feed out seal (1) in direction of arrow.

Fold back screw plate (2) in direction of arrow.

Release screws (3) and feed out door retainer (4) to inner door panel.

Installation:
Seal (1) must not be damaged.





Installation:
To install door retainer (1), fold back screw plate (2) in direction of arrow.
