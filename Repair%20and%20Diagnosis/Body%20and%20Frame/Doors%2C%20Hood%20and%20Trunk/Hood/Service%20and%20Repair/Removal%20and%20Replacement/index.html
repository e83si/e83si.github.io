Removal and Replacement




41 61 000 - Removing and installing engine hood





Refer to the description texts of the KSD CD (FRU 41 61 000) for details of stripping-down and rigging work.





Warning!
Support engine hood in fully opened position with suitable apparatus. Risk of injury.





Note:
Carry over schematic representation to the relevant vehicle type.

Loosen screws (1).
Unfasten screws (2).
Remove hood lid.

Installation Note:
Install engine hood at screw locations to on hinge. This dispenses with the need for adjustment after installation.

If this is not possible, adjust engine hood Adjustments.
Tightening torque 41 61 1AZ [1][2]41 61 Engine Bonnet/Hood.
