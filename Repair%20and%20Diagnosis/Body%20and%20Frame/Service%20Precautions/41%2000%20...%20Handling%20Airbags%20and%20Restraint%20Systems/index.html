41 00 ... Handling airbags and restraint systems

1.0 Airbags and restraint systems
- On vehicles with airbags and restraint systems, observe the relevant safety instructions [1][2]Safety Regulations For Handling Airbag System Components.
- During straightening work on the body, you will be exposed to the risk of accidental airbag triggering when the battery is connected.
Due to this potential hazard, it is essential to disconnect Instructions for Disconnecting and Connecting Battery the battery negative lead prior to welding and straightening work. Prior to disconnecting the negative lead, interrogate the fault memories of the control units if necessary.
