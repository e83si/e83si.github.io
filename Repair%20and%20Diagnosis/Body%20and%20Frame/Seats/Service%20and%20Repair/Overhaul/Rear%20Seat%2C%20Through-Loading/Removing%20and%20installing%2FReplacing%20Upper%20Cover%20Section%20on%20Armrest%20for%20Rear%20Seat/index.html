Removing and installing/Replacing Upper Cover Section on Armrest for Rear Seat




52 26 033 - Removing and installing/replacing upper cover section on armrest for rear seat





Drive out two-part hinge pin (1) with a suitable tool (bar, etc.), pressing material in area (2) on both sides (risk of damage).

Installation:
Drive in one hinge pin each from right and left. Hinge pins must be flush with outer edge of hinge.
