Removing and Installing/Replacing Rear Head Restraint (Through-Loading System), Left or Right (Compact)




52 26 390 - Removing and installing/replacing rear head restraint (through-loading system), left or right (Compact)





Fold back head restraint and move upwards to max. extent.
If necessary, press gently on guides towards front and rear and pull out firmly.
