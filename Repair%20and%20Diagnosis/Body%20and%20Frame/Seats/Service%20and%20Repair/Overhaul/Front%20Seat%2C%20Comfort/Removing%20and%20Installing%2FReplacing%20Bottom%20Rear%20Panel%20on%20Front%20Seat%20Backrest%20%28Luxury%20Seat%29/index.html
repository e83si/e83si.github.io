Removing and Installing/Replacing Bottom Rear Panel on Front Seat Backrest (Luxury Seat)




52 17 202 - Removing and installing/replacing bottom rear panel on front seat backrest (luxury seat)

Headrest must be moved as far forwards as possible.
Remove rear panel at top. Removing and Installing/Replacing Top Rear Panel on Front Seat Backrest (Luxury Seat)





Remove clips (1) with side cutting pliers.
Unclip rear panel (2) first at side towards rear, then lift out at rear.





Installation:
Clips (1) and springs (2) must be correctly seated in rear panel mounts and must not be bent.
If necessary, replace defective clips/springs.
