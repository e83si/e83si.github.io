Air Bag(s) Arming and Disarming: Service and Repair



Failure to comply with warning and repair instructions may result in faulty activations and cause physical injury!

Inspection, testing and installation work may only be carried out by properly qualified personnel.

Work on components of the airbag system must always be carried out with the battery disconnected, the negative pole or terminal covered and the plug connector of the wire leading to the gas generator disconnected. If the battery is only disconnected, it is absolutely essential to adhere to the prescribed waiting period:

- 30 minutes for vehicles up to 9/93
- 1 minute for vehicles from 9/93

Always read and following Safety regulations for handling air bag components. See Service Precautions. [1][2]Safety Regulations For Handling Airbag System Components
