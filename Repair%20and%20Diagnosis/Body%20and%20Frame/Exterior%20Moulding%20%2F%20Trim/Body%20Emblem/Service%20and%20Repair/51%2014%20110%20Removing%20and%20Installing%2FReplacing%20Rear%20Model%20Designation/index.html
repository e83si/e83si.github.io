51 14 110 Removing and Installing/Replacing Rear Model Designation




51 14 110 - Removing and installing/replacing rear model designation





Important!
The "Instructions for number/letter combination cementing 51 14 ... Instructions For Attaching Number/Letter Designation Combination" serve as the basis for these repair instructions and must be followed without fail.





Stick on top edge of adhesive tape (1) to bottom edge of rear lid (2) at distance "A" and "B".
Mark measurement "C" from side edge (3) measured on top edge of adhesive tape.
Dimensions:
A= - 50 mm
B= - 55 mm
C= - 77 mm

Note:
Measurements "D" and "E" are for information purposes only, as they are determined by carrier film.
D= - 15.6 mm
E = - 170 mm
