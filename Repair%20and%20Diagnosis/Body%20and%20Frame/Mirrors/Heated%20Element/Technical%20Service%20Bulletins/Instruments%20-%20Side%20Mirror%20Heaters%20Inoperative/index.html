Instruments - Side Mirror Heaters Inoperative

SI B51 11 05
October 2006

Body Equipment
Technical Service

This Service Information bulletin supersedes S.I. B51 11 05 dated July 2005.

[NEW] designates changes to this revision

SUBJECT
Side View Mirror Heating Operation

MODEL
The following vehicles have side view mirrors with LIN-bus technology:
E46/3 and E46/4 (3 Series Sports Wagon and Sedan) produced from 3/03

E46/2 and E46/C (3 Series Coupe and Convertible) produced from 9/03

E83 (X3 SAV) from start of production

[NEW] The E85 (Z4) has been removed from the affected model list.

SITUATION
The side view mirrors do not heat after the ignition is turned on.

CAUSE
Change in mirror heating operation for vehicles produced with LIN -bus side view mirrors.

CORRECTION
Verify the mirror operating temperature.

PROCEDURE
Do not replace any parts until the operating temperatures listed below are verified.
