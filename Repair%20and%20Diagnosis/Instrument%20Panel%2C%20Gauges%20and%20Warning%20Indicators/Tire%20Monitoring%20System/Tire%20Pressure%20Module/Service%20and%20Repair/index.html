Tire Pressure Module: Service and Repair




36 11 105 - Removing and installing RDC control unit





Important!
Read and comply with notes on protection against electrostatic damage (ESD protection) 61 35 ... Notes on ESD Protection (Electro Static Discharge).





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove right luggage compartment trim panel
- Disconnect negative battery cable Disconnecting and Connecting Battery Negative Lead





Press sound-insulating material (1) to one side slightly.
Release screw (2) and carefully swing out control unit holder a little.





Disconnect plug connection (1).
Press lug (2) in direction of arrow and pull out control unit (3) towards top.





Replacement:
- Carry out programming/coding Programming and Relearning
