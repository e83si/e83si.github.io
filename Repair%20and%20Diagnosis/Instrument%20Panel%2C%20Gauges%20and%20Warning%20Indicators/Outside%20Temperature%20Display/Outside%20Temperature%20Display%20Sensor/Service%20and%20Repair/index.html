Outside Temperature Display Sensor: Service and Repair




65 81 060 - Removing and installing/replacing external temperature sensor





Special tools required:
- 64 1 020 64 1 020 Release Hook





Expand mount for outside temperature sensor with special tool 64 1 020 64 1 020 Release Hook and feed out outside temperature sensor (1) in direction of arrow.

Pull out ambient temperature sensor (1).

Disconnect plug connection and remove ambient temperature sensor (1).

Installation:
Make sure ambient temperature sensor (1) engages correctly in its mount.
