A/C - Center Dash Vent Inoperative/Rattles

SI B 64 02 07
Heating And Air Conditioning

March 2007
Technical Service

SUBJECT
Heating and Air Conditioning System Center Vent Grille Inoperative

MODEL
E83 (X3) Vehicles produced from 8/24/03 to 1/14/07

SITUATION

The customer states that the center vent grille thumbwheel is inoperative and/or the center vent rattles.

CAUSE

Poor manufacturing of the fresh air grille center

PROCEDURE

Replace the center vent grille assembly as per Repair Manual instructions RA 64 22 162.

PARTS INFORMATION

Part Number Description Quantity

Refer to EPC Fresh air grille center 1

WARRANTY INFORMATION

Covered under the terms of the BMW New Vehicle Limited Warranty.

Defect Code: Refer to KSD

Labor Operation: Refer to KSD

Labor Allowance: Refer to KSD
