Removing and Installing/Replacing Servodrive for Right Fresh-Air Flap




64 11 807 - Removing and installing/replacing servodrive for right fresh-air flap





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove right glovebox with housing 51 16 366 Removing and Installing Right Glovebox With Housing





Release screws (1) and remove servomotor (2) complete with bracket.





Disconnect plug connection (1).
Disengage actuating lever (2) and remove bracket with servomotor (3).





Replacement:
Release lock (1) and remove servomotor (2) from holder.





Installation:
Lug (1) must be adapted to fit in counterpiece (2) on holder.
