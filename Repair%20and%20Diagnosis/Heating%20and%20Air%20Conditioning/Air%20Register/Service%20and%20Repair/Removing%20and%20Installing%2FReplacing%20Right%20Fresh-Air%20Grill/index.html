Removing and Installing/Replacing Right Fresh-Air Grill




64 22 136 - Removing and installing/replacing right fresh-air grill





Special tools required:
- 00 9 318 00 9 318 Multipurpose Wedge





Unclip right fresh-air grill (1) with special tool 00 9 318 00 9 318 Multipurpose Wedge and remove in direction of arrow.





Installation:
Catches (1) on fresh-air grill (2) must not be damaged or missing.
