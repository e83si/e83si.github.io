Back Pressure Compensation (X Series_X3-E83)




Back Pressure Compensation

Back pressure compensation
The back pressure compensation function is used to counteract the air pressure in the vehicle interior at high driving speeds. here, depending on the speed signal, the diameter of the air inlet is reduced to reduce the size of the air inlet channel.

Compensation is achieved by various flaps.

E53, E39 as from 3/2000: Back pressure flaps, left and right

E38/E46/E83 and E39 up to 3/2000: right and left recirculated fresh air flap

Back pressure compensation is run in a road speed range of 60 km/h to 160 km/h.
