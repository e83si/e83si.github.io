Blower With Rotary Switch (IHKR Integrated Heating/Air Conditioning Control E83)



Blower With Rotary Switch

Blower with rotary switch
The blower can be set in several stages with the aid of a rotary switch. Depending on the set stage, several resistor windings of the series resistor are connected in series for the purpose of reducing the voltage in the blower motor.
