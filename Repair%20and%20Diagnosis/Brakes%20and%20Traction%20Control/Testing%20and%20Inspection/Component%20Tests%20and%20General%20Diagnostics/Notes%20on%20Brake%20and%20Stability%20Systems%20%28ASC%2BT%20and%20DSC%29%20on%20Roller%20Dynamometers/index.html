Notes on Brake and Stability Systems (ASC+T and DSC) on Roller Dynamometers




34 00 009 - Notes on brake and stability systems (ASC+T and DSC) on roller dynamometers





Note:
Running BMW / MINI vehicles on dynamic brake analyzers is governed by different instructions, depending on the vehicle model.

Important!
In the case of all-wheel drive vehicles, power measurements on single-axle roller dynamometers are generally not permitted as total failure of the transfer case cannot be ruled out during such measurements.

It is essential to follow further instructions and measures for brake testing in accordance with the special instructions in Appendices 1 to 9 for the vehicles in question:
1. E36, E46, E38, E39, E52

2. E53

3. E60, E61, E63, E64, E65, E66, E67

4. E83 Checking Brakes on Test Stand

5. E85, E86

6. E70, E71

7. E90 / E91/ E92

8. E60, E61

9. R50, R52,R53, R55, R56
