Front Axle




31 33 100 - Removing and installing/replacing coil spring for left or right front spring strut





Important!
Both coil springs on the relevant axle must be replaced only in the event of corrosion breakage!

Note:
The coil spring is allocated in the Electronic Parts Catalog (EPC) under the menu item "Spring table" after the vehicle identification number has been entered and the relevant optional extra of the car has been selected.





Note:
Procedure is described in the document "Replacing front left or right spring strut Replacing Front Left or Right Spring Strut Shock Absorber".
