Removing and Installing/Replacing Front Stabilizer




31 35 000 - Removing and installing/replacing front stabilizer





Warning!
Danger to life!
Secure engine in installation position to prevent it from falling down.





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove both stabilizer links from stabilizer Removing and Installing/Replacing Push Rod (Stabilizer Link) for Left/Right Stabilizer
- Lower front axle support Lowering/Raising Front Axle Carrier





Release screws (1).
Remove both retaining brackets (2) and rubber mount (3).
Remove stabilizer sideways through wheel arch.

Installation Note:
Check both rubber mounts (3) for damage, replace if necessary.
Keep retaining bracket (2), rubber mount (3) and stabilizer clean and free from oil and grease.
Tightening torque 31 35 1AZ Front Axle - Stabilizer Bar.





After installation:
- Carry out steering angle sensor adjustment Adjustments
