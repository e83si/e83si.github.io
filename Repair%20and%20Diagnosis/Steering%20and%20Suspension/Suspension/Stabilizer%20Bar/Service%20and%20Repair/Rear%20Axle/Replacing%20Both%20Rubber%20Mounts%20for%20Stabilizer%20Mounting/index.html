Replacing Both Rubber Mounts for Stabilizer Mounting




33 55 021 - Replacing both rubber mounts for stabilizer mounting





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove emergency wheel pan Service and Repair





Release screws (1).
Tightening torque 33 55 3AZ Rear Axle - Stabilizer Bar.
Turn stabilizer in downwards direction.
Remove retaining bracket (2) and rubber mount (3).

Installation:
Keep retaining bracket (2), rubber mount (3) and stabilizer clean and free from oil and grease.
