Replacing Stabilizer Links for Stabilizer




33 55 040 - Replacing stabilizer links for stabilizer





Special tools required:
- 31 1 140 31 1 140 Open-End Wrench WAF 16/18





Release nuts (1, 2); grip with special tool 31 1 140 31 1 140 Open-End Wrench WAF 16/18 if necessary.

Remove stabilizer link (3).

Installation:
Replace self-locking nuts.

Tightening torque 33 55 7AZ Rear Axle - Stabilizer Bar.
