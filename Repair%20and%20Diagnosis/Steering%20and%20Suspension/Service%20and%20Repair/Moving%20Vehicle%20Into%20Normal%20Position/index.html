Moving Vehicle Into Normal Position




32 00 ... - Moving vehicle into normal position





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Check compliance with test conditions Test Conditions for Chassis/Wheel Alignment Check, bring vehicle to a stop if necessary.





- Version with air spring system: Carry out ride-height-calibration
- Check vehicle interior and luggage compartment (incl. luggage compartment recess) for load, unload vehicle if necessary
- Introduce DIN load (refer to Technical Data Normal Position) into vehicle
- Determine vehicle ride height
Note: If the vehicle ride height is not inside the tolerance (refer to Technical Data), the vehicle must be repaired (coil spring/leakage).
