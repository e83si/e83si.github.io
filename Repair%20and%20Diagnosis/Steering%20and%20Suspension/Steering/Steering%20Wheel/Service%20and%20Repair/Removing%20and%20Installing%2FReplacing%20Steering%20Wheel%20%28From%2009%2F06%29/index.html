Removing and Installing/Replacing Steering Wheel (From 09/06)




32 33 000 - Removing and installing/replacing steering wheel (from 09/06)





Note:
Wheel/chassis alignment is not necessary after the steering wheel has been removed and installed or replaced.





Necessary preliminary tasks:
- Remove airbag unit Removing and Installing/Replacing Airbag Unit (From 09/06)





Move wheels/steering wheel into straight-ahead position.
Release screw (1) and remove steering wheel (2).

Installation:
Tightening torque 32 33 1AZ [1][2]Specifications.





Installation:
Align steering wheel by way of marking (1) to steering column marking (2) and attach.





Replacement:
- Modify multifunction steering wheel covers/switches Replacing Left or Right Cover for Steering Wheel (From 09/06)
